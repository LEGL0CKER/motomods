Why MotoMods Minis

Big bike energy. Tiny footprint.

Every mini is modeled from the real geometry of the full-size bike, then printed and finished one at a time.

Printed, Not Molded

Each mini is 3D-printed at 0.08mm layers — crisp spokes, real suspension detail, no injection-mold seams.

True-to-Scale

Modeled from real frame geometry at roughly 1:24 scale. Wheelbase, rake, and stance match the bike in your garage.

Your Colorway

Pick frame, rim, and seat colors to match your actual build. Custom number plates available on request.

Articulated Details

Bars that turn, wheels that spin. Deluxe editions add working suspension linkage in printed titanium-tone PLA.

Display Ready

Every mini ships on a printed display stand with a laser-etched model plaque. Desk, shelf, or dash — it belongs there.

Break It? We Reprint It

Snapped a lever off your mini? Send a photo and we'll print replacement parts for the cost of shipping.

About

Made by a rider, for riders.

MotoMods started when our founder couldn't find a decent model of his Ultra Bee anywhere — so he spent a month modeling one and printed it on his desk.

Now it's a one-man print farm shipping minis to riders everywhere. Every model is drawn from scratch, every print is checked by the same hands that ride the real thing on weekends.
