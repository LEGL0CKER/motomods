Process

Print bed to shelf.

[ Avg. turnaround — 6 days ]

Nothing sits in a warehouse. Every order starts as spools and resin.

01

Modeled in-house

Every bike drawn from real frame geometry. If we haven't ridden it, we don't sell it.

02

Resin + FDM print

Fine detail cures on resin printers; structural parts run on FDM. 6–9 hours per bike.

03

Hand-finished

Sanded, assembled and detailed by hand. Every wheel spun, every bar turned before boxing.

04

Boxed to survive

Foam-lined display box, model plaque, tracking number. Arrives broken? We reprint it. Free.

About

Built by
riders.

MOTOMODZ started in a garage with one Ultra Bee, one 3D printer and one obsession: shrink the bikes we love down to something that looks unreal on a shelf.

Every model is designed in-house, printed on resin and FDM machines, hand-finished, and shipped in a foam-lined display box. If it's on our site, we've ridden the real one — and we got the details right.
