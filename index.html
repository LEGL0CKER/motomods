Specs

Built like the real thing.

Four stages between our print farm and your shelf.

01 /

Modeled in-house

Every bike is drawn from real frame geometry. Wheelbase, rake, and stance verified against the full-size machine.

02 /

Resin + FDM print

Fine detail parts cure on resin printers; structural parts run on FDM. 40+ components per bike.

03 /

Hand-finished

Sanded, assembled, and detailed by hand. Wheels spin, bars turn, suspension compresses on deluxe builds.

04 /

Boxed to survive

Foam-lined display box with model plaque. If it arrives broken, we reprint it. No questions.

About

Built by riders.

MOTOMODZ started in a garage with one Ultra Bee, one 3D printer and one obsession: shrink the bikes we love down to something that looks unreal on a shelf.

Every model is designed in-house, printed on resin and FDM machines, hand-finished, and shipped in a foam-lined display box. If it's on our site, we've ridden the real one — and we got the details right.
