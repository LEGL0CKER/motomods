Why MotoMods

Manufacturing is the feature.

Traditional frames are welded to an average rider. Ours are printed to exactly one: you.

🖨️

Printed to Fit

Send us your measurements and riding style. Our lattice-optimized frames are generated per-rider and printed in-house on industrial composite printers.

♻️

Recycled Composite

Frames use recycled carbon-nylon feedstock. Crash a frame? Send it back — we regrind and reprint at 40% off.

⚡

Serious Power

750W–2000W mid-drive motors, torque-sensing pedal assist, and swappable battery packs rated up to 100 miles.

🔧

Mod Everything

Every mount point is parametric. Racks, fenders, phone mounts, cargo pods — download the files or order printed parts.

🛰️

Smart by Default

GPS anti-theft, OTA firmware, and ride analytics in the companion app. Your bike gets better after you buy it.

🛡️

5-Year Frame Warranty

Each frame is CT-scanned before shipping. If it fails in normal riding, we print you a new one. Simple.

About

One garage. One printer. One idea.

MotoMods started when our founder crashed his commuter ebike and got quoted 12 weeks for a replacement frame. He printed his own in a weekend — and it rode better.

Today we run a small print farm and ship custom frames across the country. We're not trying to be the biggest bike company. We're trying to be the one that fits you.
